We offer comprehensive study resources for Semesters 1 through 8 of the KTU 2024 curriculum:

Semesters 3-8: These comprise department-specific subjects. Simply:

  1. Select your academic department from the available options

SGPA Calculator

Our SGPA Calculator enables you to accurately determine your Semester Grade Point Average:

  1. Select your academic branch from the dropdown menu
  2. Choose your current semester
  3. Input your subject grades (A+, A, B, C, etc.)
  4. Click "Calculate SGPA" to view your results
  5. Use the "Reset" option to clear all entries

New Feature: You can now download your SGPA calculation results as a PNG image for your records or sharing purposes.
